About us


We are Newman Games! Founded by John Newman in November 2022, Newman Games is an online store dedicated all of the latest video game releases. For years, John has been passionate about video games.

He began growing sick of physical game retailers running out of stock, and the expensive shipping fees associated with purchasing games online. Here at Newman Games, we will guarentee you will NEVER have to pay for shipping fees!

We hope you are excited as we are, as we usher in a new era of gaming!
